## Other Documented Issues (Not Fixed)

### "Kompetitiv & Fair"
**Location:** Homepage, feature card

**Current:** "Kompetitiv & Fair"
**Alternative:** "Wettbewerbsorientiert & Fair"

**Reason not changed:** "Kompetitiv" is an anglicism but acceptable for the gaming audience. It's shorter and has a modern feel that matches the gaming context.

---

### "Match-Format"
**Location:** Silver Age information page

**Current:** "Match-Format"
**Alternative:** "Spielformat"

**Reason not changed:** "Match" is widely understood in gaming contexts and is commonly used in German gaming communities. This is a minor style preference.
